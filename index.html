Quality We Use

Our chef use local products, from dairy to fruits, from flour to frosting. For each cup cake baked here, exact quantity and amount of ingredients is used, so everytime you taste it, you get the same mouth watering taste and aroma. We prepare everything in small quantities , so nothing gets wasted or overdone and each day we make fresh breads and start from scratch. We work with all the safety norms and guidelines for the pandemic. Regular temperature checks, masks, gloves are handy to us.
